<b>toArray</b>
The toArray() operator in RxJS is used to collect all emitted values from an observable and emit them as a single array once the source observable completes. It's often useful when you want to accumulate values over time but need the entire collection at once after the observable finishes.

<i>Syntax:</i>
toArray(): OperatorFunction
