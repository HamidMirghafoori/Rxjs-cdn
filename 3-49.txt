<b>skip</b>
The RxJS skip() operator is used to skip a specified number of emissions from an observable and pass through the remaining ones. This is useful when you want to ignore a certain number of values at the beginning of the observable stream

<i>Syntax:</i>
skip(count): observable
count: The number of times, items emitted by source Observable should be skipped.

Checkout to the question commit of skip branches, follow instructions written for skip function.
