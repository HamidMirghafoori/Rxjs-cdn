<b>timer</b>
The RxJS timer() operator is used to emit values after a specified delay and completes. It can optionally emit values at regular intervals thereafter.

<i>Syntax:</i>
timer(dueTime, interval?): Observable
dueTime:  The initial delay in milliseconds before the first emission
interval: (optional): The interval in milliseconds between subsequent emissions after the first one.
