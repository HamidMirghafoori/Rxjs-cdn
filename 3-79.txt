<b>noop</b>
The noop operator in RxJS isn't an actual operator but rather a utility function provided by RxJS. The term "noop" stands for "no operation," meaning it performs no action.
It is often used as a placeholder callback for events you want to ignore or defer handling.

<i>Syntax:</i>
noop()
