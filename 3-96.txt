<b>exhaustAll</b>
The exhaustAll() operator in RxJS is used to handle observables that emit observables (higher-order observables). It works by subscribing to the first emitted inner observable and ignoring any subsequent inner observables until the current one completes

<i>Syntax:</i>
exhaustAll(): observable

Checkout to the question commit of exhaustAll branches, follow instructions written for exhaustAll function.
