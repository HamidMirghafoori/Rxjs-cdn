<b>pipe</b>
The pipe() operator in RxJS is a method used to compose and chain multiple RxJS operators together to work with an Observable stream. It allows you to apply a series of transformations or side effects to an Observable in a readable and functional way.

<i>Syntax:</i>
pipe(...functions): observable
